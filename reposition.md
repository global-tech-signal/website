# Website Evolution PRD

## Global Tech Signal — Website Positioning & Messaging Evolution

**Version:** 1.0

**Project Type:** Website Evolution (NOT Redesign)

**Owner:** Global Tech Signal

**Primary Objective**

Evolve the existing Global Tech Signal website from a technology-focused consultancy into a business outcome-focused Operational Intelligence platform.

This is **not** a redesign project.

The current website already has:

* Excellent design
* Modern UI
* Good UX
* Strong engineering credibility
* Quality case studies
* Good technical content

The objective is to improve positioning, messaging, information architecture, and user journey—not visual design.

---

# Mission

Transform the website so visitors immediately understand:

> Global Tech Signal helps businesses eliminate operational friction by designing intelligent business systems powered by AI, automation, connected software, and data.

Technology should become the mechanism.

Business outcomes should become the product.

---

# Critical Constraints

## DO NOT

❌ Redesign the website.

❌ Change branding.

❌ Change colors.

❌ Change typography.

❌ Change layout unnecessarily.

❌ Remove existing services.

❌ Remove existing case studies.

❌ Remove existing technologies.

❌ Turn the website into a generic AI agency.

---

## KEEP

✅ Current design system

✅ Existing branding

✅ Existing illustrations

✅ Existing case studies

✅ Existing services

✅ Existing engineering credibility

✅ Existing technical depth

# Strategy

The website currently communicates two different stories.

Story One:

Technology

* AI Automation
* CRM
* SaaS
* Websites

Story Two:

Business Problems

Example:

Outgrowing Spreadsheets

The second approach is significantly stronger.

The entire website should evolve toward this philosophy.

Every major page should begin with:

Business Problem

↓

Business Impact

↓

Operational Challenge

↓

Business System Solution

↓

Technology

Never reverse this order.

---

# Core Positioning

The website should consistently reinforce one idea:

Global Tech Signal builds intelligent business systems that help companies scale.

Technology is the implementation.

# Success Criteria

The new website should ensure that visitors immediately understand:

✓ GTS helps businesses scale operations.

✓ Technology is only part of the solution.

✓ AI supports business systems.

✓ Operational challenges are addressed before technical implementation.

✓ Every service is connected to measurable business outcomes.

✓ The messaging consistently reinforces one strategic narrative across the homepage, services, solution pages, case studies, and content.

---

# Final Instruction to the AI Agent

This is an evolution project—not a redesign.

Preserve the current design language, branding, and technical credibility.

Improve the website by making every page follow the same philosophy already demonstrated in the "Outgrowing Spreadsheets" solution page:

Start with the business problem.

Explain the operational impact.

Present the business system.

Then introduce the enabling technologies.

Every change should reinforce the idea that Global Tech Signal helps organizations operate more intelligently through connected business systems, AI, automation, and data—without making technology the centerpiece of the story.
